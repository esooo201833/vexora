LOGO UPLOAD INSTRUCTIONS
=========================

To display your custom Vexora logo throughout the website:

1. Upload your logo image file to this folder (public/images/)
2. Name the file exactly: vexora-logo.png
3. Recommended specifications:
   - Format: PNG with transparent background
   - Size: At least 200x200 pixels
   - Aspect ratio: Square or wide rectangle

The logo will automatically appear in:
- Header navigation bar
- Site footer
- Favicon (when properly configured)

Until the logo is uploaded, the site displays a stylish "V" placeholder with gradient colors matching the Vexora brand.

If you have a different logo format or filename, you can update the reference in:
- src/components/Navbar.jsx
- src/components/Footer.jsx
